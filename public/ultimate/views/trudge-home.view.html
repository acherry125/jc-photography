Who We Are

Trudge is RPI's one and only men's ultimate team. We have been around since sometime before 1975, right around the time ultimate was just a little baby. Its now 2014 and, according to dog years, ultimate is a teenager. Join us in helping ultimate enjoy "the best years of its life" and maybe you'll have a chance to re-live your youth while you're at it.

We are constantly searching for athletic people to play some competitive ultimate on our A and B teams. If you don't know what ultimate is all about, come out to some of the practices. We travel all around the Capital District and attend weekend tournaments throughout the Northeast to compete with other colleges such as Clarkson, Binghamtom, Albany, Union, Ithaca, and Geneseo, just to name a few. Check out our schedule for upcoming tournaments, so you don't miss out on any of our goodness.

Interested Students
